## Bottom Navigation Analysis

The bottom nav has **5 tabs**:

| Tab | Label | Links to | Icon |
|-----|-------|----------|------|
| 1 | **Home** | `/dashboard` | House |
| 2 | **Learn** | `/lessons` | Graduation cap/layers |
| 3 | **Quran** | `/mushaf` | Book |
| 4 | **Practice** | `/practice` | Clock/checkmark |
| 5 | **Profile** | `/profile` | Person |

### Nav Assessment
- вњ… **Good tab choices** вЂ” covers the core loop: Home в†’ Learn в†’ Read в†’ Practice в†’ Profile
- вњ… **Consistent** вЂ” bottom nav appears on all main pages (dashboard, recite, practice, progress, sheikh, etc.)
- вљ пёЏ **Missing from nav:** `/settings` has no bottom nav (OK, it's a sub-page of Profile)
- вљ пёЏ **Dashboard nav anomaly:** When on `/dashboard`, the SSR HTML shows only a single "Profile" tab in the pre-hydration state. The full 5-tab nav appears in the streamed content (`<div hidden id="S:0">`). This means there's a brief flash of a 1-tab nav before hydration completes.

### Nav Label Mismatch (Minor Bug)
The first tab's `aria-label` says "Go to Dashboard" but the visible label says "Home". Not a UX problem but an a11y inconsistency.
